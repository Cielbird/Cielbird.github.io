### Out of Controls

<iframe frameborder="0" src="https://itch.io/embed/697773?border_width=4&amp;bg_color=32435a&amp;fg_color=ffffff&amp;link_color=ff2020&amp;border_color=a9b8d1" width="558" height="173"><a href="https://yaboicielbird.itch.io/out-of-controls">Out Of Controls by YaBoiCielbird, TKDR</a></iframe>

A game based on the idea that the payer's controls are their health-bar. The controls can be disabled by enemies, and when all the controls are destroyed, the player dies. I designed the UI and coded most of the game logic. The game was designed and developed with Unity 3D in less than 48 hours in a team of two for the GMTK 2020 game jam. The theme was chosen by GMTK.

## Unity Demos

### Heightmap Errosion
<iframe width="420" height="315"
src="https://youtube.com/embed/5saPWiJieFc">
</iframe>
This Unity project simulates simple errosion on a heightmap. It procedurally generates a mesh based off of the heightmap information. 

### Procedural Trees
<iframe width="420" height="315"
src="https://youtube.com/embed/X-mxJ1pkKm0">
</iframe>
This Unity project procedurally generates the mesh of a tree, then procedurally generates a skeleton and rigs it to the mesh. Then, physics and colliders are applied to the skeleton, allowing it to interact with the surrounding world. This video shows several of these procedural trees being destroyed by a physics sphere.

### Destructible Planet
<iframe width="420" height="315"
src="https://youtube.com/embed/UqktSJVWqr0">
</iframe>
This Unity project uses the marching cubes algorithm to make a destructible planet. Since this algorithm would be extremely slow on this scale, the project uses parallel computing on the GPU (with compute shaders) to acheive quick framerates. The player's movement is also made to rotate and "gravitate" towards the planet's center.
